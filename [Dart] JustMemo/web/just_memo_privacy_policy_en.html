The Company does not collect, store, or process any personal information through the JustMemo app.

Article 2 (Provision and Entrustment of Personal Information)

The Company does not provide personal information to third parties and does not entrust any external entity with personal information processing.

Article 3 (Retention and Use Period of Personal Information)

Since the Company does not collect or store any personal information, there is no retention or usage period.

Article 4 (Destruction of Personal Information)

The Company does not retain personal information; therefore, there is no information subject to destruction.
If a user removes the app, any memo data stored on the device will be deleted according to the user’s device settings.
